I work with each client’s innate strengths in order to provide skills to improve life long after therapy has terminated. Our approach to therapy is problem oriented and solution focused. Our therapeutic intervention techniques are informed by cognitive behavioral and attachment theories and we utilize scientific, evidence-based treatments to ensure the optimal care for our clients. We work with individuals to identify ways to alter thoughts, behavior, and environment to improve and change old patterns and lead to a more positive emotional experience.
